Drug-to-antibody ratio (DAR)

Determine average DAR by any of three orthogonal methods. Pick the method you ran — the inputs and formula update to match.

DAR = (ελmax,mAb − R·ε280,mAb) / (R·ε280,LP − ελmax,LP), R = Aλmax / A280

Enter absorbance read errors to propagate a 95% confidence interval onto the DAR. Leave blank to skip.

Conjugation Designer

Plan a conjugation reaction from antibody mass and reagent equivalents. Computes moles of antibody (and free thiols where relevant), reagent stock volumes, reaction volume at the target antibody concentration, and cosolvent top-up. Route presets carry the protocol defaults.

n(mAb) = mass / MW V(reagent) = (equiv · n_ref) / C_stock V_rxn = mass / [mAb]

Ellman's assay — free thiols per antibody

DTNB reacts with free sulfhydryls to release TNB (ε412 = 14150 M⁻¹cm⁻¹). The free-thiol-to-protein ratio confirms the degree of reduction before conjugation.

free thiols = (A412 / ε412) / (A280 / ε280,mAb)

Definitions & terms

Concise reference for the quantities and assays used throughout this calculator. Every formula named here is implemented once in the shared calculation core.

DAR drug : antibody
Drug-to-antibody ratio — the average number of payload molecules conjugated per antibody. The central critical quality attribute (CQA) of an ADC; measurable by UV/Vis, HIC, or LC-MS.
ε280 M⁻¹cm⁻¹
Molar extinction coefficient at 280 nm — how strongly a species absorbs UV light at 280 nm. Antibody concentration is derived from A280 via Beer–Lambert once ε280 is known.
ελmax M⁻¹cm⁻¹
Molar extinction coefficient of the payload at its absorbance maximum (λmax). Combined with Aλmax, it isolates the payload signal from the antibody backbone.
CF@280 correction factor
Ratio ε280 / ελmax for a payload — the fraction of its λmax absorptivity that also appears at 280 nm. Used to subtract the payload's 280 nm contribution when solving for DAR.
A280 / Aλmax absorbance
Measured absorbance (AU) at 280 nm and at the payload λmax. Their ratio R = Aλmax / A280 drives the two-wavelength UV/Vis DAR deconvolution.
HIC chromatography
Hydrophobic-interaction chromatography — separates ADC species by drug load. Peak areas give the fraction at each integer DAR; the average DAR is the area-weighted mean.
LC-MS reduced vs. intact
Mass spectrometry of drug load. Reduced (denaturing + reducing): light and heavy chains are measured separately and DAR = 2·mean LC + 2·mean HC. Intact (native): the whole ADC is measured and DAR is the intensity-weighted mean drug count.
Ellman free thiols
DTNB (Ellman's reagent) assay quantifying reduced cysteine –SH groups per antibody. Confirms the reduction state before maleimide conjugation; full IgG1 reduction exposes 8 interchain thiols.
Equivalents & basis
Reagent amount expressed as a multiple of a reference: per mAb (relative to moles of antibody) or per free thiol (relative to reactive thiols). Sets the stoichiometry of each addition in the conjugation plan.
Process yield
Recovered ADC mass as a percentage of starting antibody mass — how much material survived conjugation and purification. Paired with the formulation volume needed to reach a target concentration.
One calculation core. Each definition above corresponds to a function in adc_core (Python + JS + this page), pinned to shared golden values so the numbers never drift between platforms.
